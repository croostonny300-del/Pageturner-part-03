About PageTurner Bookstore

Founded 15 years ago, PageTurner Bookstore has been at the heart of Pretoria’s reading community.

We host book clubs, community events, and author signings to bring readers and writers together. Our goal is to make reading a shared adventure — whether you’re discovering a new genre or meeting your favorite author in person.
